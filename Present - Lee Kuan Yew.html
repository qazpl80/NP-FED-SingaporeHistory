Lee Kuan Yew's Contributions as Singapore's Negotiator

Relations With Other Countries

Upon hearing Mahatir Mohamad's promotion to Deputy Prime Minister of Malaysia, mr Lee would have frequent visits to improve both personal and diplomatic relationships between them. Mr Lee has also focused on the relationship of The United States and China by making sure that the West understands China and likewise the explain the United States in all its contradictions to the Chinese to ensure that both countries do not misunderstand each other.

Political Legacy

Mr Lee's accomplishments in Singapore had a noticeable effect the Communist leadership in China, who sent over 22,000 Chinese officials to Singapore to study and emulate his policies of economic growth. Other World leaders such as Former British Prime Ministers Margaret Thatcher and Tony Blair and more notably former American President Barack Obama who referred to him as "legendary figure of Asia in the 20th and 21st centuries".
